Grandma's Secret Apple Pie

This apple pie recipe has beenn in my family for generations and never fails to delight


Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 6 medium-sized apples,peeled and sliced

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a bowl, mix the sugar and cinnamon.
  3. Toss the appls in the sugar-cinnamon mixture.
